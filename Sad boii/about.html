My story

SIBONELO BLESSING SIBISI (SB) - My Story Sadboii isn’t only about being sad but it’s deeper than that. Way deeper, the label looks at all of the things that make you, you. You might not have woken up and thought the way you did a couple of years before today but they are surely different.

In other words being a sadboii isn’t only about being all in your emotions and bumming people out with your sad stories but I look at it as a person who has gone through something in their life that projects their train of thought to a greater plane in life.

That loss, heartbreak, emotional awareness, discovery and ultimately sadness is that breaking point in everyone’s life where you had to go through a deep cycle of introspection that lead to you going for your goals and seeing the vision more clearly. That’s what a sadboii is to me.
